I am a Bioinformatician at NCT Berlin - German Cancer Research Center (DKFZ), working on cancer genomics and personalized medicine. I lead multi-omics analyses of rare cancer cohorts, integrating whole exome sequencing (WES), whole genome sequencing (WGS), and RNA-seq data for clinical variant discovery.

I completed my PhD in Bioinformatics at the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology, Leipzig, where I developed computational methods for ancient DNA analysis. My first-authored tools : [KIN](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13059-023-02847-7) for genetic relatedness estimation and a [joint PCA/F-statistics framework](https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.09.25.615036), are widely used and cited in archaeogenetics research.

My work has been published in **Nature**, **PNAS**, and **Genome Biology**.

## Research Interests
- Cancer genomics and personalized medicine
- Multi-omics data integration (WES, WGS, RNA-seq)
- Statistical methods for clinical variant analysis
- Population genetics and ancient DNA
